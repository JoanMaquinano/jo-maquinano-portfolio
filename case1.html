Mortgage Trading Analysis

Power BI Case Study — Trade Pricing and Premium Analysis

Project Overview

This project simulates the role of a junior trader working on a mortgage trading desk. Using mortgage, loan balance, and bid data, the analysis focuses on evaluating trade prices and determining whether bids are trading at a premium or discount relative to the underlying loan balances.

Business Context

Mortgage trading involves buying and selling pools of loans based on expected cash flows and risk. Traders must quickly understand how bid prices compare to outstanding principal balances to identify attractive trades.

This case study mirrors a real-world trading workflow, requiring both clean data modeling and accurate financial calculations to support decision-making.

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • DAX
  • Power Query

Analytical Approach

  • Cleaned and transformed mortgage and loan balance data
  • Created relationships between bid, loan, and balance tables
  • Calculated trade prices using related loan balance values
  • Derived trade premiums to identify premiums and discounts
  • Analyzed trade outcomes using tabular and summary visuals

Key Outcome

The final model enabled clear comparison between bid prices and scheduled principal balances, making it easy to identify trades executed at a premium versus those at a discount. This provides a practical foundation for evaluating trade attractiveness in a mortgage trading environment.
