Problem

Raw data contained duplicates, missing values, cancellations, and non-positive transactions, making it hard to derive reliable business insights without a robust cleaning and analytics pipeline.

Approach

  • Cleaned data and engineered `Revenue` metrics.
  • Built executive overview (trends, geography, product performance).
  • Implemented RFM scoring and segment logic.
  • Developed a 2-page interactive Streamlit dashboard.

Key Findings

  • Revenue is concentrated in a few geographies and product groups.
  • Late-year periods show stronger revenue performance, indicating seasonality.
  • A high-value customer segment drives a large share of total revenue.
  • At-risk customers represent a major opportunity for win-back campaigns.

Business Recommendations

  • Prioritize retention and personalized engagement for high-value segments.
  • Launch structured reactivation campaigns for at-risk customers.
  • Optimize inventory and promotions around top-performing product groups.
  • Use country-specific strategies for pricing, promotion, and assortment.
