About Me

I’m a data analyst with a foundation in scientific research and a track record of building reliable, decision-ready datasets. I hold a BS in Biology (Zoology) from the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB), where I developed strong skills in analytical thinking, hypothesis-driven work, and rigorous data handling.

At Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS), I validate and quality-check sustainability data for more than 5,000 companies. My work centers on data integrity, consistency checks, and structured QA workflows, delivering 99%+ accuracy in high-stakes reporting environments.

I focus on turning complex, imperfect data into trusted insights that stakeholders can act on through careful validation, clear analysis, and business-oriented storytelling.
