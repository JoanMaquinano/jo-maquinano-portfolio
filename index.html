About Me

I hold a BS in Biology major in Zoology from the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB), with formal training in scientific research and long‑term ecological analysis. When field research was disrupted during the pandemic, I transitioned into data work, applying the same rigor, skepticism, and methodological discipline to structured datasets.

I am currently an Analyst in the Data team at Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS), where I validate and quality‑assure sustainability data for over 5,000 companies. My work involves applying structured data collection and interpretation methods across high‑volume ESG datasets. Through systematic data verification and structured cross‑checks, I consistently deliver 99%+ accuracy in high‑stakes reporting environments.

In parallel, I develop my technical skill set through hands‑on projects in SQL, Python, AI, Power BI, and Tableau, and through independent data research. I am particularly drawn to roles that prioritize data integrity, traceability, and sound interpretation over surface‑level metrics.

Outside of my day‑to‑day role, I trade in the forex market and research real estate secondary‑market data, building simulations and backtests to understand risk, valuation, and long‑term patterns. I continue studying in my free time because data skills compound, and curiosity allows for growth.

My goal is to contribute to credible data systems that generate meaningful insights, support business decisions, and create real-life positive impact.
