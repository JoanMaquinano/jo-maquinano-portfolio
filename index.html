About Me

I hold a BS in Biology (Zoology) from the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB), with formal training in scientific research and data analysis. When field research was disrupted during the pandemic, I transitioned into data work, applying the same rigor and methodological discipline to structured datasets.

I am currently an Analyst at Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS), validating sustainability data for over 5,000 companies. My work focuses on structured verification and cross‑checks, consistently delivering 99%+ accuracy in high‑stakes environments.

My goal is to contribute to credible data systems that support sound decision‑making and real‑world impact.
