About Me

In my current role at Institutional Shareholder Services, I validate and quality‑assure sustainability data for 5,000+ companies, applying technical screening criteria, DNSH principles, and minimum safeguards across high‑volume datasets. I consistently deliver 99%+ accuracy by combining rigorous source verification, systematic cross‑checks, and clear methodological reasoning, ensuring outputs that stakeholders can trust in high‑stakes reporting environments.

My background in scientific research and long‑term ecological data analysis informs how I approach ESG data: with precision, skepticism, and respect for methodological nuance. I have conducted decade‑scale analyses using R, translating complex environmental datasets into insights that withstand academic and regulatory scrutiny. This foundation allows me to bridge technical depth and clarity, turning raw data into reporting‑ready intelligence.

I am particularly drawn to work that demands discipline over dashboards, where accuracy, transparency, and thoughtful interpretation matter more than surface‑level metrics. My goal is to contribute to data systems and frameworks that support credible sustainability decisions, not just compliance.
