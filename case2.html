Mortgage Trading Analysis

Simulated a mortgage trading desk workflow to evaluate trade pricing and identify premium/discount opportunities using Power BI and financial logic.

Power BI DAX Power Query

Project Overview

This case study models the role of a junior trader analyzing mortgage bids against outstanding principal balances to assess trade attractiveness.

Business Context

Mortgage trading requires fast and accurate valuation checks. The dashboard supports this by making trade pricing and premium/discount status immediately visible for decision support.

Analytical Focus Areas

Analytical Approach

  • Cleaned and transformed mortgage, bid, and loan balance datasets.
  • Built a relational model across bid, loan, and balance tables.
  • Created DAX measures for trade pricing and premium calculations.
  • Flagged premiums and discounts for quick trade evaluation.
  • Designed summary visuals for desk-level decision support.

Key Outcome

The final model enables clear and reliable comparison of bid prices against principal balances, helping users quickly identify favorable and unfavorable trades. The dashboard forms a practical base for market-side valuation review workflows.
